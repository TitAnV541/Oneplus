OnePlus - Never Settle

OnePlus Technology (Shenzhen) Co., Ltd.doing business as OnePlus, is a Chinese consumer electronics manufacturer headquartered in Shenzhen, Guangdong. It is wholly owned by Oppo, which is in turn a subsidiary of BBK Electronics along with Vivo, Realme and iQOO.OnePlus mobile phones are available in 34 countries. This Chinese smartphone manufacturer is known for offering high-end features at affordable prices compared to other brands in the same segment.It wouldn’t be unfair to say that OnePlus was once a bootstrapped startup but has progressed to become a global sensation. So much so that the OnePlus success story is taught as a case study for aspiring managers at business schools.Known as ‘Flagship killers’, OnePlus mobile phones have come a long way.

Here is a list of the top 7 features that make a OnePlus smartphone unique.
1. Vibrant displays
Earlier, Samsung dominated the Indian and overseas smartphone market with its patented SUPER AMOLED displays. But things have changed now. The latest OnePlus mobile phones, such as OnePlus 8T and OnePlus 8T+ 5G, have 120 Hz Fluid AMOLED displays with an aspect ratio of 20:9. These displays are HDR10+ ready and not available in most other phones in this price range.
2. Latest features
As a brand, OnePlus has been a quick adopter of industry trends and has even helped to set them. These days, a OnePlus phone comes with best-in-class hardware and appealing form factors. For example, OnePlus 8T and OnePlus 8T+ feature 8 GB/ 12 GB RAM and 128 GB/ 256 GB internal storage. OnePlus 8T+ also comes with a Qualcomm SM8250 Snapdragon 865 5G processor and an Adreno 650 GPU, which is a powerhouse in itself. All OnePlus phones offer a wide range of connectivity options, so you never have to worry about keeping in sync with the world around you.
3. Top-notch cameras
OnePlus mobiles offer great main and selfie cameras. These handsets have multiple rear and front cameras with features such as dual-LED flash, HDR, panorama, 4k recording and gyro-EIS (stabilization). The camera in your OnePlus smartphone is one of its highlights. It will help you click the snazziest of photos that will be the envy of everybody. OnePlus 8T, for instance, comes with a quad main camera setup with a 48MP main lens, as well as a 16MP selfie camera.
4. Long battery life
You can expect OnePlus phones come with a battery capacity of at least 4,500 mAh. This means your phone will last you a whole day without needing a recharge. OnePlus phones are known for their solid battery life and fast charging support, which is why so many users prefer them. OnePlus 8T, OnePlus 8T+, and 0ne Plus 9RT come with a 4,500 mAh battery, a class-leading feature. Additionally, the OnePlus 8T supports 65W fast charging that fully charges the device in just 40 minutes.
5. Flamboyant sound
If you are big on music, OnePlus mobile phones are just a handset for you. Premium models like OnePlus 8T come with punchy stereo speakers that sound crisp and loud. With such good speakers on your phone, you will find listening to music and watching movies an immersive experience. These dual stereo speakers have noise cancellation and Dolby Atmos support. Seamless Bluetooth connectivity means that you can easily sync your OnePlus smartphone to any external speaker. OnePlus devices are compatible with various audio formats like MP3, AAC, AAC+, WMA, WAV, FLAC, MID, M4A and many more.
6. Good phones in all price brackets
One Plus is one of the few emerging smartphone manufacturers that have their presence across customer segments. Therefore, it is easy for you to pick a OnePlus mobile phone according to your spending power yet not compromise on any features. The main reason why OnePlus has established a name for itself with Indian consumers is its keenness to provide maximum innovation at the lowest price. Whether you are looking for a mid-range phone or a premium device, the OnePlus has just the right mobile for you.
7. Special protection plans
OnePlus knows that even the best phones are prone to drops and accidents. Having the fastest processor or 8 GB RAM on your mobile will not prevent it from being damaged if it falls on the floor or into a steaming bowl of soup. Keeping this in mind, OnePlus offers extended protection plans that will continue to take care of your device long after the standard warranty period is over. These plans cover damages due to drops, breakages and liquids. Opting for one of these plans is a sure-shot way of keeping your OnePlus mobile safe and sound.
Summary:
OnePlus has established itself as a strong contender in the Indian smartphone market. OnePlus mobile phones are known for their excellent features and affordable prices, so they are highly coveted by consumers. OnePlus 8T is currently one of the most popular phones in our country and comes with class-leading specifications in an appealing form factor. If you are looking for more reasons to pick up a OnePlus phone, this article will give you all the information you need to make an intelligent choice.
